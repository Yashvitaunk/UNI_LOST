How to Use Lost & Found System

  • Login using your registered Email & Password or Mobile & OTP.
  • After login, you can Report Lost Items with details like item name, description, and location.
  • You can also Report Found Items so others can claim them.
  • Use the search feature to check if someone reported your lost item.
  • If you find your item listed, you can contact the finder through the platform.
